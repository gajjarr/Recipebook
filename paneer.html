Delicious Paneer Sandwich

Ingredients For Paneer Filling

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese) - 200 grams, crumbled or grated
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 1 small, finely chopped (optional)
  • Capsicum (Bell Pepper) - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Green Chilies - 1-2, finely chopped (optional, for spice)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder - 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Garam Masala - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Coriander Leaves - a handful, chopped

Ingredients for Sandwich

  • Bread Slices - 6-8 slices (white, whole wheat, or multigrain)
  • Butter - for spreading on bread
  • Green Chutney - 2-3 tablespoons (optional, for added flavor)
  • Cheese - grated (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Tomato Ketchup - for serving (optional)

Additional Ingredients

  • Chaat Masala - a pinch for tangy flavor
  • Lettuce Leaves - for crunch (if making a cold sandwich)
  • Mayonnaise - as a spread

Steps to Prepare

  1. Crumbled Paneer: Grate or crumble 200 grams of fresh paneer (cottage cheese) into a bowl.
  2. Sauté Veggies: In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil. Add finely chopped onions, capsicum (bell pepper), and optional tomatoes. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables soften slightly.
  3. Add Spices: Add 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon garam masala, and salt to taste. Stir to combine.
  4. Mix Paneer: Add the crumbled or grated paneer to the pan with the vegetables and spices. Mix well to combine everything evenly. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat.
  5. Toast Bread: Take 4 slices of bread and spread butter on one side of each slice. You can optionally spread green chutney or mayonnaise. Toast the bread on a pan or in a sandwich maker until golden brown.
  6. Assemble: Place 2-3 tablespoons of the prepared paneer filling on one slice of bread. Top it with another slice to form a sandwich. You can add grated cheese or extra herbs for flavor.
  7. Serve: Slice the sandwich into halves or quarters. Serve hot with chutney, tomato ketchup, or a dip of your choice.

Enjoy your homemade Paneer Sandwich!
